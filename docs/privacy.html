Privacy Policy

Review It — Chrome extension
Last updated: March 2026

This policy describes how the Review It browser extension (“the Extension”) handles information when you use it. The Extension helps you generate a short review based on your star rating, optional notes, and context from the webpage you are viewing.

What information is collected

The Extension does not sell your data. When you click the action to generate a review, the Extension may send the following to our backend service over HTTPS:

  • Star rating you selected (1–5).
  • Optional free text you typed in the Extension popup.
  • Page URL and page title of the active browser tab (the tab you had open when you used the Extension).
  • Limited page metadata read only from that same tab, when permitted by the browser: the main heading (h1) or Open Graph title (og:title), and declared page language (for example <html lang> or language meta tags), to tailor the generated review.

The Extension uses Chrome’s activeTab and scripting permissions so this happens only when you invoke the Extension (for example by opening its popup)—not continuously in the background on sites you do not interact with through the Extension.

Where data is processed

The payload is sent to our API endpoint: https://review-it-backend-587398533610.europe-west4.run.app/api/review (hosted on Google Cloud). Our servers may call an AI provider to produce the review text.

What we do not do

  • We do not read passwords, payment data, or cookies from websites.
  • We do not scan all tabs or your full browsing history.
  • We do not run injected scripts on pages until you use the Extension on that tab as allowed by the browser.

Retention

The app will store request logs to improve maintenance and performance, the logs are anonymizes, so no user data is logged, only the rank and the hotel/restaurant/place.

User rights

You can stop sending data by not clicking generate, or by uninstalling the Extension. You may have rights under GDPR or other laws to access, correct, or delete personal data—contact us using the details above.

Contact

The Extension is provided by the developer listed on the Chrome Web Store listing. For questions about this policy, use the contact details on the store listing.
